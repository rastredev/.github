# Rastre

Rastre is a zero-knowledge audit logging platform built for multi-tenant SaaS environments where security and compliance cannot be compromised.

Rastre provides tiered data isolation with dedicated audit trails for customers operating in regulated industries. The platform implements customer-managed encryption (BYOK), allowing organizations to maintain complete control over their encryption keys. This ensures that audit data remains encrypted with customer-provided keys both in transit and at rest &mdash; making it impossible for Rastre or any third party to access or decrypt sensitive audit logs.

The platform automatically detects and redacts personally identifiable information while maintaining cryptographic protection against tampering or manipulation of audit records. Rastre's architecture supports environment-based segmentation across sandbox and production deployments, with compliance-driven data partitioning designed specifically for organizations subject to regulatory requirements in finance, healthcare, government, and other highly regulated sectors.
